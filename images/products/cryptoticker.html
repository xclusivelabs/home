What is CryptoTicker?

CryptoTicker is a small, always-on Wi-Fi connected display that pulls live cryptocurrency price data and shows it at a glance. No phone, no browser — just the numbers you care about, always visible on your desk.

Configure your preferred coins and refresh interval through the built-in web interface. Once set up, it runs completely standalone.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which cryptocurrencies are supported?
CryptoTicker supports any coin available through the configured price API (default: [Placeholder]). Popular coins like Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), and others can be selected through the web configuration interface.
Does CryptoTicker require an internet connection?
Yes. CryptoTicker connects to your Wi-Fi network and fetches live price data. It requires a 2.4 GHz Wi-Fi network and an active internet connection to display prices.
How do I configure my Wi-Fi and coin selection?
On first boot, CryptoTicker creates its own Wi-Fi access point. Connect to it from your phone or computer, then open the configuration page in a browser. Enter your Wi-Fi credentials and choose your coins. The device will save the settings and connect automatically on reboot.
How often does the price update?
The refresh interval is configurable in the web interface. The default is every [Placeholder] seconds. Note that very short intervals may be rate-limited by the price API.
Can I change the price API or add custom data sources?
Yes. Because the firmware is open source, you can modify it to pull from any compatible REST API. Advanced users can build and flash custom firmware builds.
