What is StockBeacon?

StockBeacon is a dedicated Wi-Fi connected display built for stock market watchers who want market awareness without constantly checking their phone or switching browser tabs. Select your watchlist, set it on your desk, and let it do the watching for you.

It cycles through your chosen stocks automatically, showing everything you need to make informed decisions at a glance — price, change, and the indicators that matter most. Price data is sourced from Yahoo Finance and is subject to their standard delay.

Screen Modes

Overview Screen

The overview screen shows all your selected stocks side-by-side — current price and 24-hour change for each. A quick glance tells you where your watchlist stands right now.

Per-Stock Detail View

Cycle through each stock to see its full detail screen. Every stock gets its own view with a price chart and key technical indicators displayed clearly.

Technical Indicators

Each per-stock detail view includes the following indicators:

  • EMA — Exponential Moving Average to spot trend direction
  • Bollinger Bands (BB) — Volatility bands showing price extremes relative to the mean
  • MACD — Moving Average Convergence Divergence for momentum signals
  • Volume — Trading volume to confirm price moves and trend strength
  • RSI — Relative Strength Index to identify overbought or oversold conditions

Candlestick Pattern Detection

StockBeacon automatically detects key candlestick patterns on the price chart, giving you instant visual signals without having to interpret the candles yourself. The following patterns are supported:

  • Hammer — Bullish reversal signal appearing at the bottom of a downtrend
  • Inverted Hammer — Potential bullish reversal at the end of a downtrend
  • Shooting Star — Bearish reversal signal appearing at the top of an uptrend
  • Doji — Indecision candle indicating a potential trend change
  • Bullish Engulfing — Strong bullish reversal where a large green candle engulfs the prior red candle
  • Bearish Engulfing — Strong bearish reversal where a large red candle engulfs the prior green candle
  • Morning Star — Three-candle bullish reversal pattern signalling the end of a downtrend
  • Evening Star — Three-candle bearish reversal pattern signalling the end of an uptrend

Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find what you're looking for? Contact us.

Which stocks can I track?
StockBeacon supports a wide range of publicly traded stocks. Select your watchlist through the browser-based configuration interface using standard stock ticker symbols.
How does the screen cycling work?
StockBeacon starts on the overview screen showing all your selected stocks at once. It then automatically cycles through each stock's detail view — showing the price chart and all indicators for that stock — before looping back to the overview. The cycling interval is adjustable in the configuration interface.
What does the overview screen show?
The overview screen shows all your selected stocks side-by-side with current price and 24-hour percentage change for each. It gives you the full picture at a single glance.
Which indicators are shown on the per-stock detail view?
Each detail view shows: EMA (Exponential Moving Average), Bollinger Bands, MACD (Moving Average Convergence Divergence), Volume, and RSI (Relative Strength Index) — alongside the price chart.
Which candlestick patterns does StockBeacon detect?
StockBeacon detects 8 candlestick patterns automatically: Hammer, Inverted Hammer, Shooting Star, Doji, Bullish Engulfing, Bearish Engulfing, Morning Star, and Evening Star. Detected patterns are highlighted directly on the price chart so you can spot signals at a glance.
Where does the price data come from? Is it real-time?
StockBeacon uses Yahoo Finance as its data source. Yahoo Finance data is delayed — prices are not real-time. This means StockBeacon is best suited for general market awareness and trend monitoring rather than time-sensitive trading decisions.
Does StockBeacon require an internet connection?
Yes. StockBeacon connects to your 2.4 GHz Wi-Fi network and fetches price data continuously via Yahoo Finance. A stable internet connection is required for price updates and indicator calculations. Note that Yahoo Finance data is delayed and stock data follows standard market hours.
How do I configure my Wi-Fi and stock watchlist?
On first boot, StockBeacon creates its own Wi-Fi access point. Connect to it from your phone or computer, then open the configuration page in a browser. Enter your Wi-Fi credentials, choose your stocks, and set your cycling speed. The device saves the settings and connects automatically on reboot.
Can I customize the firmware or data sources?
No. StockBeacon runs signed firmware to guarantee data accuracy, display stability, and reliable Wi-Fi connectivity. Custom or third-party firmware can cause incorrect price data, display glitches, or a non-functional device, and voids the warranty. All supported customization — stock selection, cycling speed, and display preferences — is available through the built-in configuration interface.
