What is PCVitals?

PCVitals is a compact, always-on display that shows your PC's live performance data without interrupting your workflow. Set it on your desk, connect it to your Wi-Fi, and let it monitor your system continuously — so you always know what your PC is doing without switching windows or opening task managers.

A lightweight companion app runs on your PC and streams system data to the display over your local network. No cloud, no subscriptions — just your stats, live on your desk.

⚠️ Compatibility: PCVitals currently requires Windows and an NVIDIA GPU. AMD and Intel GPU support is not available at this time.

Screen Modes

PC Vitals Screen

The main screen shows all key performance metrics at once — CPU usage, GPU temperature, RAM usage, network download and upload speeds, disk activity, and battery percentage. Colour-coded ring gauges make it instantly readable at a glance.

Clock & Weather Screen

Tap the screen to switch to a clean clock and weather view — showing the current time, date, temperature, and conditions. Tap again to switch back to the vitals screen at any time.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find what you're looking for? Contact us.

How does PCVitals get my PC's data?
A lightweight companion app runs on your Windows PC and streams system metrics to PCVitals over your local Wi-Fi network. No internet connection or cloud service is involved — all data stays on your local network.
Which operating systems does the companion app support?
The PCVitals companion app currently supports Windows only. Additionally, GPU temperature monitoring requires an NVIDIA GPU — AMD and Intel GPUs are not supported at this time.
What metrics does PCVitals show?
PCVitals displays CPU usage (%), GPU temperature (°C), RAM usage (%), network download and upload speeds (KB/s), disk activity (KB/s), and battery percentage — all updated live from the companion app.
How do I switch between screens?
Simply tap the display to switch between the PC Vitals screen and the Clock & Weather screen. Tap again to switch back at any time.
Does the companion app slow down my PC?
No. The companion app is designed to be extremely lightweight. It reads system metrics and sends them to PCVitals at regular intervals with minimal CPU and memory overhead — you won't notice it running.
How do I set up PCVitals for the first time?
On first boot, PCVitals creates its own Wi-Fi access point. Connect to it from your phone or computer, then open the configuration page in a browser. Enter your Wi-Fi credentials and the device will connect to your network. Then install and run the companion app on your PC — PCVitals will find it automatically.
Does PCVitals require an internet connection?
PCVitals connects to your local Wi-Fi network and communicates directly with the companion app on your PC — no internet connection is required for PC metrics. An internet connection is only needed for the Clock & Weather screen to fetch current weather data.
